Market Report: Q3 2023 Trends in Phnom Penh

The Phnom Penh real estate market has shown significant resilience in Q3 2023 despite global economic uncertainties. This report provides a comprehensive analysis of current market conditions, price movements, and demand patterns.

Price Trends

Average property prices in Phnom Penh have increased by 3.2% quarter-over-quarter, with luxury condos in central districts showing the highest growth at 5.8%. The premium segment continues to attract foreign investors, particularly from China, South Korea, and Singapore.

Demand Patterns

There has been a noticeable shift in buyer preferences, with increased demand for properties with home offices and green spaces. The rental market remains strong, with vacancy rates below 5% for quality apartments in central locations.

Future Projections

Market analysts predict continued moderate growth in the second half of 2023, driven by infrastructure development and foreign direct investment. However, rising interest rates may temper demand in the mid-range segment.
